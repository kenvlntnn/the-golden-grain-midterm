Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Find out everything you need to know about our products, wholesale services, and distribution.

What types of rice brands do you offer?

We curate only the best quality grains for our customers. Our premium selection heavily features top-tier brands. We cater to both everyday household needs and specific restaurant requirements.

Do you offer bulk pricing for restaurants and resellers?

Absolutely! Our wholesale distribution service is tailored specifically for B2B clients such as restaurants, carinderias, and local retail stores. We offer highly competitive, discounted pricing tiers for bulk orders.

How do you ensure your products are always in stock?

We utilize a custom-built Rice Distribution and Inventory Management System. This technology allows us to precisely track our stock levels in real-time, predict demand, and reorder before supplies run low, ensuring our shelves are never empty.

Aside from rice, what other products do you sell?

To be your complete daily staple provider, we also distribute farm-fresh eggs (available per tray or in bulk) and premium, clear cooking oil for both retail and wholesale customers.

Can I schedule regular deliveries for my business?

Yes. As part of our B2B Inventory Solutions, we can set up automated, scheduled deliveries based on your usage rates. We aim to act as an extension of your own business's supply chain.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, our team is ready to assist you with specific inquiries regarding pricing, availability, and partnership opportunities.
